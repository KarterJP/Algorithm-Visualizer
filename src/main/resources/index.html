The goal of this project is two-fold. The first and most important goal is to improve my understanding of algorithms, data structures, and concepts: by taking an algorithm and implementing it myself, I will be forced to research until I am fully comfortable with it. As well, the painstaking process of slowing it down and simulating it step by step should ensure a full understanding of how it operates.

The second goal of this project is to further refine my skills in JavaScript, HTML/CSS, AWS, Java, and websites and webservers.


I will continue to build upon this application for the forseeable future,
